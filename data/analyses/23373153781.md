## What to improve
- **Respect planned rest days**, especially in a cutback week and the day after quality work — this is when supercompensation actually happens. The adaptation from the VO2max session accrues during rest, not during the next bout of effort.
- If you want to move on a rest day, keep it true Z1–Z2: stretching, an easy walk, or gentle cycling at avg HR ~100–110. That promotes blood flow without adding load.
- **Sleep (score 53) is the highest-leverage recovery lever here.** Prioritise 7–8 hours before quality sessions and during cutback weeks; it does more than any supplement or protocol.
