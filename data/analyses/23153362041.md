## Session summary
Outdoor long run in County Dublin, **5.0 km in 24:30 at 4:53/km**, light rain ~14°C. Average HR **135** (max 148), cadence 159. Splits held steady at 4:42–5:05 before a 6:30 cooldown km.

## Versus the plan
Planned: 8.0 km easy long run at 4:50/km. Pace was right on (4:53), but the run came in **3 km short** (5.0 vs 8.0). HR 135 is appropriately conversational for a long effort — well judged in the rain.

## Effect on your profile
Solid aerobic endurance work, and valuable as your only *outdoor* run in this block — real-surface footing and weather that the treadmill can't replicate. HR stayed flat across the steady kms, confirming the pace was sustainable.

## What to improve
- **Length is the point of a long run** — at 5 km this trained like an easy run. Build back toward the full 8–10 km so the long run actually stresses endurance.
- Good pacing discipline; next time extend the steady portion rather than adding the slow 6:30 km at the end.
